About Me

Photo: REUTERS/Mark Kauzlarich

While I'm originally from Brooklyn, New York, I recently moved to Austin Texas. Before moving, I spent 20 years working in the financial sector. In that time, I’ve held jobs ranging from Help Desk Analyst up to Vice President of Information Technology. I’ve done everything from desktop support to managing a real-time equity trading and accounting platform.

I spend most of my free time tinkering with old computer hardware, hacking old video game consoles to work with my modern HD TV, and being generally disappointed in the performance of the New York Mets.
